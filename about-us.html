About Nike

Nike, the world's leading designer and marketer of athletic footwear, apparel, equipment and accessories, is a global icon known for its innovative products and inspiring athletes. From the legendary Michael Jordan to the rising stars of today, Nike has fueled generations of athletes and continues to push the boundaries of sports performance.

Our Mission

To bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world. (*If you have a body, you are an athlete.)

Innovation

Nike is constantly pushing the boundaries of athletic performance with groundbreaking innovations like Nike Air, Flyknit, and ZoomX. These technologies have revolutionized the way athletes train and compete, helping them to achieve their full potential.

Our History

Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports, Nike was initially a distributor for Japanese shoe company Onitsuka Tiger. In 1971, the company was renamed Nike and began designing and manufacturing its own footwear. Since then, Nike has grown into a global powerhouse, with a presence in over 190 countries and a diverse portfolio of brands, including Jordan Brand, Converse, and Hurley.

Sustainability

Nike is committed to making a positive impact on the environment and society. The company has set ambitious goals to reduce its carbon footprint, conserve water, and promote sustainable manufacturing practices. Nike also supports a variety of community initiatives around the world.
